# Will the Customer Accept the Coupon?

## 1. General Overview

The strongest pattern across all coupons:  
**People are much more likely to accept a coupon for a place they already go to regularly.**

## 2. Bar Coupon Findings

Key differences between acceptors and non-acceptors:

- **Visit frequency is huge**  
  - People who go to bars **never** or **less than once a month** → very low acceptance (~20–35%)  
  - People who go **1–3 times/month or more** → much higher acceptance (~60–70%)  
  → If bars aren’t already part of their routine, they usually skip the coupon.

- **Passenger matters a lot**  
  - **Alone**, **with partner**, or **with friends** → decent acceptance  
  - **With kids in the car** → acceptance drops sharply (many drivers just don’t want to detour to a bar with children)

- **Other notes**  
  - Younger drivers (under ~30) and people with certain occupations sometimes showed slightly higher rates, but the biggest driver was still **how often they normally visit bars**.

## 3. Coffee House (Cafe) Coupon Findings

Coffee house coupons were accepted more often than bar coupons, but still showed clear patterns.

Key differences:

- **Visit frequency again dominates**  
  - People who go to coffee houses **never** or **less than once a month** → lower acceptance  
  - People who visit **1–3 times/month or more** → noticeably higher acceptance rates  
  → Same story as bars: if coffee shops are already part of their life, they’re way more likely to say yes.

- **Age impact is large**  
  - **Under 21** years old → hige rise in acceptance  
  - **26 years and above** → lower acceptance, closer to total average rate

- **Other notes**  
  - Men sometimes accepted coffee house coupons at slightly higher rates than women (small difference)  
  - Income showed minor trends (higher income groups occasionally more willing), but not a strong driver
  - Occupation had a impact where jobs with longer or unusual hours (healthcare, maintenance) had higher acceptance rates

Bottom line for coffee houses: **Regular coffee drinkers and young driver** = best acceptance odds.
